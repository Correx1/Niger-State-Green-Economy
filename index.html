About Us

about our initiative

The Niger State Green Economy initiative is a groundbreaking and ambitious plan to transition Niger State's economy to a low-carbon, sustainable model. This bold vision aims to unlock the state's vast potential for renewable energy, climate-smart agriculture, sustainable forestry, and a circular economy, while creating a prosperous future for its residents.

our key focus

Renewable Energy

Niger State is blessed with abundant sunshine and wind, making it ideal for the development of solar and wind energy projects. The state government has already signed a Memorandum of Understanding with the Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation Limited (NNPC) to develop mega solar parks, generating clean and affordable electricity.

Climate Smart Agriculture

Niger State is a major agricultural producer, contributing significantly to Nigeria's food security. However, traditional agricultural practices often contribute to deforestation and soil degradation. The green economy plan aims to promote climate-smart agricultural practices, such as crop rotation, cover cropping, and water conservation techniques, to ensure long-term sustainability and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Circular Economy

The green economy plan promotes the development of a circular economy in Niger State. This involves minimizing waste, maximizing resource utilization, and promoting recycling and reuse. Initiatives include promoting waste-to-energy projects and establishing industrial parks that focus on circular economy principles.

Green Jobs

The transition to a green economy is expected to create millions of new jobs in Niger State. The government is committed to providing training and support for workers in new green industries.

Sustainable Forestry

Niger State is home to extensive forests, which play a vital role in regulating climate, protecting biodiversity, and providing livelihoods for local communities. The green economy plan emphasizes the importance of sustainable forest management, including afforestation programs and community-based forest management initiatives.

Past Event Highlight

Niger State Green Economy Summit, led by Governor Alhaji Mohammed Umaru Bago, aiming to establish the state as Nigeria's Green Economy hub. The Niger State Committee on the Green Economy (NSCGE) oversees climate integration, project monitoring, and collaboration with federal agencies. Key objectives include creating a sustainable development blueprint, hosting a multi-stakeholder Green Summit, and advising on financing. Sponsorship opportunities were detailed, and stakeholder were encouraged to reserve spots, emphasizing sustainability and collaboration for Niger State's economic and environmental prosperity.
